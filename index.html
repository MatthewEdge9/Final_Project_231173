MAC MILLER


Circles is the sixth and final studio album by American rapper and singer Mac Miller. It was released posthumously on January 17, 2020, by REMember Music and Warner Records, and was being worked on by Miller before his death in September 2018. This album's main theme is being directionless. Mac communicates his feelings of being unable to break out of his bad habits and make progress in his life.
